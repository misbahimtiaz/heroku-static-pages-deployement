About us


Usually, it is difficult to order flowers and cakes online because of cake weight selection and flavors selection. Moreover, the variety isn’t usually available in Pakistan. But Once Upon A Cake website has made it easier for Karachite customers and those living in the US, UK, UAE, Saudia Arab, Canada, or anywhere in the world. Now, select your designs and order as many pounds as you wish while selecting your favorite flavors and Cake Topper. We already cover more than 10 flavors and are regularly adding more. Aside from variety in flavors and flexibility in choosing weight, OUAC becomes a one-stop-shop if you intend to throw a nice party. Our products include cakes, cupcakes, cake-pops, cakesicles, cookies, and macrons. This list will keep on updating.
